# EMAIL AI LAB
Engineering Minds in Artificial Intelligence Learning
Purdue University Northwest

EMAIL AI Lab is an engineering-focused student laboratory dedicated to the study and development of artificial intelligence systems through mathematics, computational engineering, and applied research.

Our goal is to develop engineers who understand AI at the fundamental level — from linear algebra and optimization to embedded intelligence and autonomous systems.

---

## Focus Areas

• Machine Learning & Deep Learning
• Mathematical Foundations of AI
• Embedded AI Systems
• Autonomous Systems and Robotics
• Computational Engineering

---

## Current Activities

• Weekly technical lectures on AI and machine learning
• Applied engineering projects
• Drone autonomy research
• Mathematical foundations workshops
• Collaborative coding and research

---

## Get Involved

EMAIL is open to engineering students interested in building real technical depth in AI and computational systems.

Members participate in lectures, engineering projects, and research collaboration.
