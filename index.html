About the Lab

Built for engineers.
Driven by curiosity.

EMAIL AI Lab was founded on a simple conviction: building great AI systems requires deep fluency in mathematics, hardware, and systems thinking — not just calling APIs.

Members work on real problems: deploying neural networks on microcontrollers, building autonomous navigation systems, and developing computer vision pipelines from first principles. The lab operates with the rigor of a professional research group and the energy of a founding team.
